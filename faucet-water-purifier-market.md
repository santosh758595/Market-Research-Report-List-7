<p><p>The faucet water purifier market consists of three primary types: faucet-mounted, counter top, and built-in purifiers. Faucet-mounted purifiers attach directly to the faucet for straightforward installation and easy access to clean water. Counter top units are standalone systems placed next to the sink, offering larger filtration capacity and versatility. Built-in purifiers are integrated into the plumbing system, providing seamless filtration while saving counter space, though they often require professional installation. Each type caters to varying consumer preferences and needs.</p></p>
