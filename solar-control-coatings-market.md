<p><p>Solar control coatings are designed to manage the amount of solar energy that enters a building or vehicle. The market is categorized into three main types: metal coatings, alloy coatings, and metal compound coatings. Metal coatings typically utilize materials like silver or aluminum to reflect solar radiation effectively. Alloy coatings combine different metals to enhance performance and durability. Metal compound coatings incorporate compounds such as oxides or nitrides to provide selective light transmission while reducing heat gain, optimizing energy efficiency.</p></p>
