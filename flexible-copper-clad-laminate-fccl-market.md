<p><p>Flexible Copper Clad Laminate (FCCL) is a crucial material in electronics, providing a substrate for flexible printed circuits. The FCCL market includes various manufacturing techniques: Tape Casting involves forming thin layers of copper on flexible substrates; Sputtering applies a thin metal coating through a vacuum process; Electroplating deposits copper onto a substrate electrochemically, enhancing conductivity; and Laminating bonds multiple layers of materials, improving mechanical and thermal properties. Each method addresses specific application needs in the evolving landscape of flexible electronics.</p></p>
