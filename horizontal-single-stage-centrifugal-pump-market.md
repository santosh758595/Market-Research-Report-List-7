<p><strong>The Horizontal Single Stage Centrifugal Pump Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Self-priming Centrifugal Pump</li><li>Non-self-priming Centrifugal Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The horizontal single-stage centrifugal pump market can be categorized into two main types: self-priming and non-self-priming centrifugal pumps. Self-priming centrifugal pumps can draw liquid into the pump without needing manual priming, making them ideal for applications where the pump might be above the liquid source. In contrast, non-self-priming centrifugal pumps require the pump casing to be filled with liquid before operation, commonly used in situations where the pump is submerged. Each type serves distinct operational needs across various industries.</p></p>
