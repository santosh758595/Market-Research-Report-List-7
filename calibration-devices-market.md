Electrical calibration equipment focuses on verifying electrical parameters like voltage and resistance. Physical/dimensional calibration equipment ensures accurate measurements of dimensions, such as length and mass. Thermodynamic calibration equipment assesses temperature, pressure, and flow measurements, ensuring proper function in thermal systems. Each type plays a crucial role in maintaining accuracy and compliance across various industries.</p></p>
