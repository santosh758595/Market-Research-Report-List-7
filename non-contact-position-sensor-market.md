<p><strong>The Non Contact Position Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Linear Position Sensor</li><li>Rotary Position Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The non-contact position sensor market comprises two primary types: linear and rotary position sensors. Linear position sensors detect displacement along a straight path, often using technologies like capacitive, magnetic, or optical sensing. Rotary position sensors, on the other hand, measure angular displacement or position around a pivot point, employing methods such as Hall effect or inductive sensing. These sensors are widely used across industries, including automotive, aerospace, and robotics, due to their precision, durability, and minimal wear mechanisms.</p></p>
