<p><p>A Membrane Aerated Biofilm Reactor (MABR) is an innovative wastewater treatment technology that combines biological treatment processes with membrane filtration. It utilizes a semi-permeable membrane to facilitate the transfer of oxygen directly to a biofilm growing on its surface, allowing for efficient removal of organic matter and nutrients from wastewater. MABR systems promote enhanced biodegradation under aerobic conditions while minimizing energy consumption compared to traditional aeration methods.</p><p>The MABR market is experiencing significant growth, driven by increasing global concerns regarding water scarcity and the need for sustainable wastewater management solutions.
