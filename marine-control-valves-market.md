<p><p>The marine control valves market comprises various types, including ball valves, butterfly valves, and others. Ball valves provide quick shut-off and high flow capacity, making them ideal for on-off control in marine applications. Butterfly valves are known for their lightweight design and ability to handle large volumes of fluid with minimal pressure drop, suitable for throttling control. Other types may include gate, globe, and check valves, each serving specific functions in marine systems, thus enhancing operational efficiency and safety.</p></p>
