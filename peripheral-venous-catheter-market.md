<p><p>The peripheral venous catheter market comprises three main types: temporary catheters, short-term catheters, and long-term catheters. Temporary catheters are designed for brief use, typically in emergency situations or for short procedures. Short-term catheters are utilized for routines lasting up to several days or weeks, while long-term catheters are intended for extended use, often for chronic conditions requiring frequent venous access. Each type serves distinct clinical needs, impacting patient care and healthcare settings in varying durations and purposes.</p></p>
