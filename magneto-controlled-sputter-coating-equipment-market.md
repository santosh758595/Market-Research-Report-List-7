<p><p>Magneto-controlled sputter coating equipment encompasses various power types, each tailored to specific applications. DC powered systems are ideal for conducting materials, offering efficient deposition rates. RF powered units accommodate insulating materials, enabling uniform coating by utilizing radio frequency energy. MF powered (Medium Frequency) systems blend both capabilities, allowing effective deposition on both conductors and insulators by using a frequency range that enhances performance. Each type plays a critical role in industries like electronics, optics, and materials science.</p></p>
