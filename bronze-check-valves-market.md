<p><strong>The Bronze Check Valves Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solder End Connections</li><li>Threaded End Connections</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The bronze check valves market can be categorized into two main types based on their end connections: solder end connections and threaded end connections. Solder end connection valves are designed for permanent applications, providing a strong, leak-proof joint through soldering, ideal for plumbing and HVAC systems. Threaded end connection valves feature male or female threads, allowing for easy installation and removal, making them suitable for applications where frequent maintenance or adjustments are necessary. Both types ensure efficient backflow prevention in fluid systems.</p></p>
