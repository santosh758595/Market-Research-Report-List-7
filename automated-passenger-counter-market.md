<p><p>The Automated Passenger Counter (APC) market consists of different types, primarily categorized as Infrared, Stereoscopic Vision, and Other technologies. Infrared systems use heat or light beams to detect passenger movement, offering cost-effectiveness and simplicity. Stereoscopic vision employs dual cameras to capture depth and accurately count passengers by analyzing visual data. Other methods may include laser-based and pressure-sensitive systems. Each type provides varying levels of accuracy, complexity, and application suitability, catering to diverse transportation needs and environments.</p></p>
