<p><strong>The Titanium Oxychloride Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Sulfate Process</li><li>Chloride Process</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Titanium Oxychloride market is primarily segmented into two processes: sulfate and chloride. The sulfate process involves the reaction of titanium ore with sulfuric acid, which yields titanium dioxide and other byproducts. Conversely, the chloride process utilizes chlorine gas and petroleum coke to produce titanium tetrachloride, which is then converted into titanium oxychloride. Each process varies in terms of efficiency, cost, and environmental impact, influencing market dynamics and applications across industries such as pigments, ceramics, and aerospace.</p></p>
