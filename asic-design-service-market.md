<p><strong>The ASIC Design Service Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Standard Cell Based ASIC</li><li>Gate Array Based ASIC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ASIC design service market primarily comprises two segments: standard cell-based ASICs and gate array-based ASICs. Standard cell-based ASICs utilize a predefined library of logic cells to create custom integrated circuits tailored for specific applications, offering flexibility and high performance. In contrast, gate array-based ASICs start with a generic chip layout, allowing for customization of functionality by configuring the interconnections. While standard cell-based designs provide greater design freedom, gate array-based solutions can reduce time-to-market and development costs.</p></p>
