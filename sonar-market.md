<p><p>Sonar markets encompass various systems designed for underwater exploration and mapping. A Single Beam Scanning Sonar System uses a single sonar beam to measure depths, ideal for straightforward depth profiling. Multi-beam Sonar Systems deploy multiple beams simultaneously, creating detailed 3D maps of the seafloor. Side Scan Sonar Systems produce images of the seafloor by emitting sonar waves at an angle, excellent for object detection. Other market types may include advanced sonar technologies like synthetic aperture sonar and imaging sonar for specialized applications.</p></p>
