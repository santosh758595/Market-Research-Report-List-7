<p><strong>The Infrared Thermal Imaging Temperature Measurement System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Temperature Measuring Camera</li><li>Temperature Measuring Blackbody</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The infrared thermal imaging temperature measurement system market comprises two primary types: temperature measuring cameras and temperature measuring blackbodies. Temperature measuring cameras capture thermal radiation emitted from objects, converting it into temperature readings, which are essential for non-contact monitoring in various applications. Temperature measuring blackbodies, on the other hand, are reference sources that emit a known amount of thermal radiation at specific temperatures, ensuring accurate calibration for thermal cameras, thus enhancing measurement precision in critical processes.</p></p>
