<p><p>Rotary dryers are essential for efficiently drying various materials. The market is divided into directly heated and indirectly heated types. Directly heated rotary dryers utilize hot gases to transfer heat directly to the material, offering quick drying but requiring careful management of temperature and gas composition. Indirectly heated dryers, on the other hand, heat the drum via external sources, ensuring no direct contact with combustion gases, making them ideal for sensitive materials. Each type serves specific industrial needs.</p></p>
