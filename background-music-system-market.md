<p><p>The Background Music System market is categorized into two main types: Central and Split systems. </p><p>Central systems consist of a single, centralized source that distributes audio to multiple zones or areas, providing uniform sound quality and control. In contrast, Split systems feature decentralized setups, where individual components are installed in different locations, allowing for independent operation and tailored audio experiences in each zone. Both types cater to diverse environments, such as retail, hospitality, and corporate spaces, enhancing ambiance and customer experience.</p></p>
