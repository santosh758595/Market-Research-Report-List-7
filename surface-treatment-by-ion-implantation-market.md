<p><p>Surface treatment by ion implantation involves altering the properties of materials through the controlled introduction of ions to enhance their performance, durability, and resistance to wear. The two primary market types are ion implantation and plasma injection. Ion implantation focuses on embedding ions directly into the material's surface, leading to improved hardness and corrosion resistance. In contrast, plasma injection utilizes a plasma state to deliver ions, enabling uniform coating and treatment over complex geometries, offering versatility for various applications.</p></p>
