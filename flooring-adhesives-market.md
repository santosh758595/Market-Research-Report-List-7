Polyurethane adhesives offer flexibility and durability, making them suitable for wood flooring. Urethane adhesives are known for their moisture resistance and versatility in various flooring materials. Vinyl adhesives are specifically formulated for vinyl flooring, ensuring optimal adhesion and performance.</p></p>
