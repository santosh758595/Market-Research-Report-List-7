<p><p>Carpet backing materials are crucial for the durability and performance of carpets. Primary backing, often made from synthetic fabrics like polypropylene or polyester, provides stability and support to the carpet fibers. It allows for easy installation and enhances resistance to wear. Secondary backing materials, such as jute or woven polypropylene, add strength and dimensional stability. They also help in moisture control and increase the carpet's overall lifespan, contributing to the aesthetic appeal and functionality of flooring solutions.</p></p>
