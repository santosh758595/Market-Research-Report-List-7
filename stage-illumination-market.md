<p><p>The stage illumination market primarily consists of three types: LED, halogen, and discharge lighting. LED lights are favored for their energy efficiency, longevity, and versatility in color options, making them ideal for modern productions. Halogen lights offer bright, intense illumination but consume more power and generate heat. Discharge lighting, including metal halide and xenon bulbs, provides powerful, consistent brightness and is often used in large venues. Each type serves distinct needs, balancing performance with energy consumption and cost.</p></p>
