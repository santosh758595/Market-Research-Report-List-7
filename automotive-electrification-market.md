<p><p>The automotive electrification market includes several vehicle types. Internal Combustion Engine (ICE) vehicles rely solely on gasoline or diesel. Micro-Hybrid Vehicles feature start-stop technology to improve efficiency. Hybrid Electric Vehicles (HEVs) combine an ICE with an electric motor, enhancing fuel economy. Plug-In Hybrid Electric Vehicles (PHEVs) have larger batteries, allowing for all-electric driving over short distances before switching to hybrid mode. Battery Electric Vehicles (BEVs) operate fully on electricity, offering zero tailpipe emissions and requiring charging infrastructure.</p></p>
