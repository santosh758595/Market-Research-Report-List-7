<p><ul><li>Surface Mount</li><li>Through Hole</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The single-layer capacitor market is segmented into two main types: surface mount and through hole. Surface mount capacitors are designed for direct mounting on the surface of printed circuit boards (PCBs), enabling efficient use of space and automatic assembly processes. In contrast, through-hole capacitors require holes drilled into the PCB for insertion, providing robust electrical connections that can withstand mechanical stress. Each type serves distinct applications and preferences in manufacturing, impacting overall performance and reliability in electronic devices.</p></p>
