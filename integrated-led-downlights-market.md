<p><p>The Integrated LED Downlights market features two main types: Surface Mount and Flush Mount. Surface Mount downlights are designed to be installed on top of ceilings, offering a modern aesthetic and easy installation. They typically project light outward and are ideal for spaces with limited ceiling height. Flush Mount downlights, on the other hand, are recessed into the ceiling, providing a streamlined look. These fixtures create a seamless finish and are suitable for both residential and commercial applications, enhancing energy efficiency and illumination.</p></p>
