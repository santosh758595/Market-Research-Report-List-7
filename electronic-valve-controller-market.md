<p><p>The electronic valve controller market comprises three primary types: pneumatic, electric, and hydraulic. Pneumatic controllers utilize compressed air to operate valves, making them suitable for applications requiring rapid response and high-speed operation. Electric controllers employ electrical signals to manage valve positioning, offering precise control and easier integration with automated systems. Hydraulic controllers use pressurized fluid for valve operation, enabling high force and heavy-duty applications. Each type serves different industrial needs, enhancing efficiency and performance in fluid management systems.</p></p>
