<p><p>The CPU fan market is primarily divided into three cooling types: air cooling, water cooling, and thermoelectric cooling. Air cooling utilizes heatsinks and fans to dissipate heat, being cost-effective and easy to install. Water cooling offers superior heat management with liquid coolant circulating through tubes and radiators, ideal for high-performance systems. Thermoelectric cooling employs peltier devices to create a temperature difference, providing precise cooling but often at higher energy consumption. Each type caters to different user needs and performance requirements.</p></p>
