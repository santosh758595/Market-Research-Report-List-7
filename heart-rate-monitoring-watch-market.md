<p><p>The heart rate monitoring watch market comprises two primary types: strapless heart rate monitors and chest strap monitors. Strapless monitors are typically integrated into fitness trackers or smartwatches, using sensors to measure heart rate from the wrist, offering convenience and ease of use. In contrast, chest strap monitors consist of a strap worn around the chest that provides more accurate heart rate readings through electrical signals. Each type serves distinct user preferences, focusing on accessibility versus precision in monitoring cardiovascular activity.</p></p>
