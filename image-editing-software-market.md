<p><ul><li>RAW Editing Software</li><li>Non-RAW Editing Software</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The image editing software market is divided into two primary types: RAW editing software and non-RAW editing software. RAW editing software is designed to process images captured in RAW format, allowing for extensive adjustments in exposure, color balance, and detail without compromising quality. In contrast, non-RAW editing software primarily handles JPEG and other compressed formats, focusing more on user-friendly features and quick edits. Both types cater to different user needs, ranging from professional photographers to casual hobbyists.</p></p>
