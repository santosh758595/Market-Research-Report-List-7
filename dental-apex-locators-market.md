<p><p>Dental apex locators are devices used to determine the location of the tooth root's apex during endodontic procedures. The market for these tools is segmented into two main types: alarm type and digital readout. Alarm type locators provide audible signals when the file reaches the apex, ensuring real-time feedback for the dentist. In contrast, digital readout locators offer visual displays of the file's proximity to the apex, allowing for precise measurements and enhancing overall treatment accuracy.</p></p>
