<p><p>The RTD (Resistance Temperature Detector) cables market is categorized primarily into 2-wire, 3-wire, and 4-wire configurations. </p><p>2-wire RTD cables offer a simple design but are less accurate due to potential resistance from leads. </p><p>3-wire cables improve accuracy by compensating for lead resistance, making them suitable for most applications. </p><p>4-wire RTD cables provide the highest precision by eliminating lead resistance entirely, ideal for critical applications in industrial and laboratory settings, ensuring reliable temperature measurements.</p></p>
