<p><p>LED upright microscopes are categorized into three main types based on the number of eyepieces: monocular, binocular, and trinocular. Monocular microscopes have a single eyepiece, offering simplicity and compact design, suitable for basic applications. Binocular models feature two eyepieces, providing enhanced depth perception and comfort for prolonged use, ideal for detailed observations. Trinocular microscopes include an additional eyepiece for camera attachment, facilitating documentation and analysis, making them popular in research and educational settings.</p></p>
