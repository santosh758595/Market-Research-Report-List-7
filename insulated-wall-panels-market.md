<p><p>Insulated wall panels are pre-manufactured building components designed to provide thermal insulation and structural support in construction projects. These panels consist of a core insulation material, typically made of polyurethane, polystyrene, or mineral wool, sandwiched between two outer layers made of various materials, such as metal or gypsum. This combination not only enhances energy efficiency but also simplifies the installation process, reducing both construction time and labor costs.</p><p>The Insulated Wall Panels Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 6.5% during the forecast period. Additionally, growing investments in infrastructural development and residential projects worldwide are propelling the market forward. </p><p>The latest trends indicate a shift toward eco-friendly materials and manufacturing processes, alongside the integration of advanced technologies like superior insulation systems and customizable panel designs.
