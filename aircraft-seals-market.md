<p><ul><li>Static</li><li>Dynamic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aircraft seals market is categorized into static and dynamic types. Static seals are designed to prevent fluid or gas leakage in stationary components, ensuring stability and durability in areas like fuselage joints and non-moving parts. In contrast, dynamic seals are used in moving components, such as engine or landing gear systems, where they accommodate motion while maintaining a seal. Both types play critical roles in enhancing aircraft safety, efficiency, and overall performance by mitigating the risks of leaks and environmental exposure.</p></p>
