<p><p>The green-roof market is categorized into three main types: extensive, semi-intensive, and intensive green roofs. Extensive green roofs feature lightweight soils and drought-resistant plants, primarily for insulation and stormwater management. Semi-intensive green roofs have deeper soil profiles allowing a wider variety of plants, including small shrubs. Intensive green roofs offer the most versatility, supporting trees and larger vegetation, requiring significant structural support and maintenance, often creating usable outdoor spaces. Each type serves different aesthetic and functional purposes.</p></p>
