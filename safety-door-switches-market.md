<p><ul><li>Mechanical Switches</li><li>Non-Contact Switches</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Safety Door Switches market comprises two primary types: Mechanical Switches and Non-Contact Switches. Mechanical Switches are traditional devices that operate based on direct physical contact, ensuring that safety interlocks activate when doors are opened. In contrast, Non-Contact Switches utilize technologies such as magnetic fields or optical sensors to detect the door's status without physical touch, enhancing durability and reducing wear. Both types are essential for ensuring worker safety in various industrial applications, preventing unauthorized access to hazardous areas.</p></p>
