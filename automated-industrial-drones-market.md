<p><p>The automated industrial drones market is categorized into three main types: fixed-wing, multiple rotor, and hybrid wing drones. Fixed-wing drones are efficient for long-range flights, utilizing aerodynamics for extended endurance. Multiple rotor drones, often called quadcopters or multirotors, excel in agility and vertical takeoff and landing capabilities, making them ideal for close-range inspections and surveys. Hybrid wing drones combine features of both fixed-wing and rotor designs, offering versatility in flight modes, making them suitable for various industrial applications.</p></p>
