<p><strong>The Rail Signalling Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CBTC</li><li>PTC</li><li>ATC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Rail signalling market types include Communication-Based Train Control (CBTC), Positive Train Control (PTC), and Automated Train Control (ATC). CBTC utilizes real-time data communication between trains and track systems, enhancing urban rail capacity and safety. PTC prevents train-to-train collisions through GPS and automated braking systems, primarily addressing safety on freight and passenger lines. ATC encompasses automatic train operations and supervision, optimizing service frequency and efficiency. Together, these technologies revolutionize rail transportation, improving safety and operational performance across various systems.</p></p>
