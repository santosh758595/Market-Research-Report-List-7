Electromagnetic induction detectors identify metal objects by measuring alterations in magnetic fields caused by conductive materials. X-ray detectors provide detailed images of packet contents, allowing for the identification of various materials, including metal. Each type serves distinct applications in security and quality control, enhancing safety in industries such as transportation and food processing.</p></p>
