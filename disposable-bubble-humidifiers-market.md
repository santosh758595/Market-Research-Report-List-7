<p><p>Disposable bubble humidifiers are medical devices designed to provide supplemental humidification for patients receiving respiratory therapy. This is particularly important in preventing airway irritation and improving patient comfort during treatments.</p><p>The Disposable Bubble Humidifiers Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 12.6% during the forecast period.
