<p><p>Brine concentration technology encompasses various methods for enhancing saline solutions. HERO (High Efficiency Reverse Osmosis) focuses on maximizing freshwater recovery while minimizing energy use. CCD (Controlled Crystallization and Distillation) leverages crystallization techniques to extract salts. VTFF (Vapor Compression and Thermal Filtration) combines evaporation and filtration to concentrate brine efficiently. MVC (Mechanical Vapor Compression) utilizes mechanical energy to compress vapor, enhancing thermal efficiency in distillation processes. Each method serves to optimize salt recovery and reduce environmental impacts in brine management.</p></p>
