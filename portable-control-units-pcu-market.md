<p><p>Portable Control Units (PCUs) are versatile devices used for monitoring and controlling temperature in various industrial processes. The market for PCUs can be categorized into two main types based on input configurations: RTD (Resistance Temperature Detector) input and Thermocouple input. RTD input units offer higher accuracy and stability for temperature measurements, making them ideal for precision applications, while Thermocouple input units are widely used due to their broad temperature range and quicker response times, suitable for diverse environments.</p></p>
