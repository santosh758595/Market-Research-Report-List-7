<p><p>The NTD single crystal rod market encompasses two primary types: NTD zone melted silicon single crystal rods and NTD Czochralski silicon single crystal rods. NTD zone melted rods are produced using zone melting techniques, ensuring high purity and optimal crystallinity, making them ideal for semiconductor applications. In contrast, NTD Czochralski rods are fabricated through the Czochralski process, which allows for the production of larger crystals at lower costs. Both types cater to various industries, including electronics, solar, and photonics.</p></p>
