<p><p>The Anti-Scratch Glass market is primarily categorized into two types: chemically-strengthened glass and sapphire glass. Chemically-strengthened glass is treated with alkali metal ions, enhancing its durability and resistance to scratches, making it ideal for everyday applications like smartphone screens. On the other hand, sapphire glass, made from synthetic corundum, offers superior hardness and scratch resistance, often used in luxury watches and high-end devices. Both types cater to increasing consumer demand for robust and long-lasting surfaces.</p></p>
