<p><p>The color-shifting materials market comprises various types, each defined by their response to different stimuli. Thermochromism materials change color with temperature variations, while photochromism materials shift color when exposed to light. Electrochromism materials alter hue with electrical stimulation, and solvatochromism materials respond to changes in solvent polarity. Cathodchromism materials change color due to electron bombardment. Additionally, other market types include materials that exhibit unique color shifts under specialized conditions. Together, these materials find applications across industries like textiles, electronics, and packaging.</p></p>
