<p><p>Battery fuel gauge ICs are essential for accurately monitoring the state of charge in battery-operated devices. The market can be categorized into two types: single-cell and multi-cell gauges. Single-cell fuel gauge ICs are designed for devices using one battery cell, providing simple and cost-effective solutions. In contrast, multi-cell fuel gauge ICs manage multiple battery cells, offering advanced energy management and balancing features for more complex applications, such as electric vehicles and larger energy storage systems. Both types enhance device efficiency and lifespan.</p></p>
