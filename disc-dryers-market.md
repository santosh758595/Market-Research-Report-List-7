<p><p>Disc dryers are classified based on technology type and mounting type. Technology-wise, they are divided into direct heated, where hot air or gas contacts the material, and indirect heated, which uses heated surfaces for drying without direct contact. Mounting types include vertical and horizontal configurations, impacting space utilization and efficiency. Vertical disc dryers are suited for limited space, while horizontal types offer easier access for maintenance and larger processing capacities, catering to diverse industrial needs in drying applications.</p></p>
