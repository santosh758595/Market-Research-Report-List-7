<p><ul><li>Suspension Type</li><li>Roller Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The vacuum plating machine market includes two main types: suspension type and roller type. Suspension type machines utilize a suspended platform to coat various substrates evenly, allowing for flexible configurations and part handling. In contrast, roller type machines employ a continuous roller system to process materials, enhancing efficiency and throughput for large-scale applications. Both types cater to different industries, including automotive, electronics, and packaging, by providing high-quality thin-film coatings and protecting surfaces from wear and corrosion.</p></p>
