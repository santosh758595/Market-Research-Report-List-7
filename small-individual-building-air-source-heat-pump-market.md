<p><p>The small individual building air source heat pump market is primarily categorized into two types: air-to-air and air-to-water systems. Air-to-air heat pumps transfer heat between the outside air and the indoor air, efficiently heating or cooling spaces through forced air distribution. In contrast, air-to-water heat pumps extract heat from the air and transfer it to water, which can be used for heating environments or providing hot water. Both systems offer energy-efficient heating solutions, suitable for residential and small commercial applications.</p></p>
