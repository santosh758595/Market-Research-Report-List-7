<p><strong>The Electronic Grade Microsilica Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Angular</li><li>Spherical</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Electronic Grade Microsilica market is primarily categorized into two types: angular and spherical microsilica. Angular microsilica, with its sharp edges, offers enhanced surface area and bonding capabilities, making it ideal for high-performance applications like semiconductors and advanced ceramics. In contrast, spherical microsilica has a smooth, round shape that provides better flowability and is often preferred in applications requiring uniformity and reduced friction, such as in electronic encapsulation and composite materials. Each type serves distinct applications within the electronics industry.</p></p>
