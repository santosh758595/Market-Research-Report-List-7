<p><strong>The Electric Vertical Take-off and Landing (eVTOL) Aircraft Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Vectored Thrust</li><li>Multirotor</li><li>Lift + Cruise</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Electric Vertical Take-off and Landing (eVTOL) aircraft market consists of three main types: vectored thrust, multirotor, and lift + cruise. Vectored thrust designs utilize rotors that can tilt for both vertical lift and forward propulsion, optimizing efficiency. Multirotor aircraft feature multiple rotors for vertical lift, offering simplicity and redundancy but often with limited range. Lift + cruise configurations separate lift and forward flight functions, integrating fixed wings for efficient cruising, ideal for longer distances and higher speeds.</p></p>
