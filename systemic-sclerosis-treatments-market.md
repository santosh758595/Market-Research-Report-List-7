<p><ul><li>Limited Cutaneous Systemic Sclerosis</li><li>Diffuse Cutaneous Systemic Sclerosis</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The systemic sclerosis treatments market is segmented into two main types: limited cutaneous systemic sclerosis and diffuse cutaneous systemic sclerosis. Limited cutaneous systemic sclerosis typically presents with skin involvement restricted to the fingers and face, leading to a slower progression. Conversely, diffuse cutaneous systemic sclerosis affects a broader area of the skin and is associated with more rapid disease progression and internal organ involvement. Treatment strategies differ, focusing on managing symptoms, improving quality of life, and addressing specific complications of each type.</p></p>
